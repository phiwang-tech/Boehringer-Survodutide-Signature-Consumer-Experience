LillyDirect and NovoCare are fulfillment platforms — optimized for patients who already know what drug they want. Survodutide enters a market that requires generating demand, not just converting it. Unbranded metabolic health quiz reaches Viv before she identifies as a GLP-1 patient. A plain-language eligibility score connects her symptoms to a treatment conversation. For Survodutide, where GI discontinuation runs above class benchmarks during dose escalation (SYNCHRONIZE-1), a reactive model is a commercial vulnerability, not just a feature gap.

NovoCare / WeGoTogether

Patient experience: titration education available on request; coaching starts after the patient surfaces a concern
Survodutide SCE

Prior side effect history shapes the response. Direction-aware support covers dose-hold and dose-reduction decisions without the patient having to initiate. Support arrives before the difficulty does.
